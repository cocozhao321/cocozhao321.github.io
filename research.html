Research and Project Experience

2016-2018

It is a twelve weeks high school graduate research program at the University of Pennsylvenia. The abstract titled “Effects of Age and Gender on Risk-Taking Across the Life Span” will present at annual meeting of Society for NeuroEconomics, Philadelphia, USA. In this project, my main job included but not limited to:

  • Launch the BART (Balloon Analogue Risk Task) task website by using HTML, CSS, and Java Script
  • Gather data from more than 1000 subjects via WeChat
  • Analyze data by using Excel and SPSS
  • Read papers with keyword gender, age, bart, and risk-taking
  • Write the abstract
  • Present the abstract in the conference
